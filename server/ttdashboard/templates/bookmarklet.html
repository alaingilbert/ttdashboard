To use this turntable statistics bookmarklet just drag the button below to your Bookmarks Bar, browse to turntable.fm and click it. You will see a statistics button on the header of TurnTable.

How to install it step by step ?

  • Drag the button in top of that page in your bookmarks bar.
  • Rename it to : "TT Stats"
  • Browse to turntable.fm and click it.
  • You will see a statistics button on the header of TurnTable.
