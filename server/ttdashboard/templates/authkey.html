Auth key

The auth key will allow ttdashboard to communicate directly with turntable.fm with your account.
This information is encrypted in our database. So even if someone can access it, your auth key will be safe.

When you got it, edit your profile and fill the "Auth key" field.
That's it, you can now import/export playlists directly to turntable.fm
(The auth key look like « auth+live+XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX » You have to get the whole thing)

How do I find my auth key ?

If you use google chrome, follow theses easy step :

  • Go on turntable.fm
  • Command + Option + J (Mac) | Ctrl + Shift + J (Windows)
  • Open the ressources section
  • Click on Cookies, turntable.fm and find your auth key.

For other browsers, find your cookies, and search for turntableUserAuth.
