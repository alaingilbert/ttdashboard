Sidespeech chrome extension (By AptGeek)

SideSpeech creates a chat room between you and the other users of SideSpeech visiting the same domain. You can now surf the web and talk with people that share common interests in real time.

Download SideSpeech

Is it available for other browsers ?

The extension also work on :

  • Firefox
  • Safari

http://sidespeech.com/

Why could it be useful for me ?

The turntable chat is limited to one room. Sidespeech removes this limit and allows you to speak with everybody who are on the same domain as you. Sidespeech is also totally anonymous. Nobody can know who you are. But you can set a nickname if you want !

Ok! that sounds good, but what if there is too many people on the domain ?

Sidespeech allows you to easily create and change rooms. So you can create a new room and invite your friends on it.
